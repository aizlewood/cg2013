<strong>So what IS rich media email?</strong>

Rich email is, frankly, something that never quite got off the ground. With the boom of Flash 5 years ago, people couldn't wait to merge the cheap, easy and effective pros of email and email marketing with the new media that was <strong>Flash</strong>. The thinking was that response rates would go through the roof if emails could display immersive and interactive creative that engaged the viewer in ways static HTML couldn't. The excitement was there, but the unfortunately the tech wasn't.
