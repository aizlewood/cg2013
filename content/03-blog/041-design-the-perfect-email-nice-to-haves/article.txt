text: 

The final step in the 10 step series, this quick tip addresses some 'good ideas' for your email campaign.

Other nice-to-haves:

Use an ‘Add this email address to your safe senders list’ message to ensure maximum open rates in the future
Personalize as much as possible, using all relevant data from contact’s record
Always use a subscription centre to allow recipients to update their contact details.
Use your ESP’s built-in forward-to-a-friend functionality to facilitate maximum message reach
Always display your unsubscribe link prominently; avoid confusing messaging
Test past results against current campaign. Benchmark
