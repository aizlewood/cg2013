title: ESP Gmail Appeal - Now on Video

----

date: 2008-04-09 06:58

----

text: 

The guys at Email Standards Project have completed the assembly of '<a title="The Gmail Grimace - ESP" href="http://www.email-standards.org/blog/entry/getting-some-gmail-attention/">the Gmail Grimace</a>', a worldwide appeal for email folks to display their feelings about Gmail's bizarre lack of CSS standards support.

Show your appreciation for the Gmail Grimace project - watch the video, run through a range of emotions, pick one, and visit the ESP site's '<a title="What you can do - ESP" href="http://www.email-standards.org/what-you-can-do/">What you can do</a>' page, and take it from there.  Really, it's the least you can do.
