Part 6 of this 10 part series, here are some quick tips on your text version and design.

Text Version and Design:

Always include a text version of your HTML email to accommodate users who prefer this format or are viewing from a mobile device.
Format and design text version for better results, instead of using one large block of undecipherable text
Keep hyperlinks short to avoid broken links after wrapping
When testing, always test both HTML and text versions for formatting and copy
