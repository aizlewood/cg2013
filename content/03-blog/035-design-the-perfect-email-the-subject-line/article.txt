Your Subject Line:

Keep your subject lines relevant and engaging
Keep it as short and sweet as this sentence
Shoot for 35 character length, maximum at 60 characters
Never use exclamation points in your subject line
Never use ‘Free’ in the subject line
Never use ALL CAPS in your subject line
Never use characters
