title: Google AdWords goes A/V

----

date: 2008-03-12 15:11

----

text: 

It's finally here - Google Adwords have introduced (to beta invite users only in the US only) TV advertising from within the Google Adwords interface.  This is on the tail-end of Google announcing on their official 'Inside AdWords' blog that they've <a href="http://adwords.blogspot.com/2008/03/google-audio-ads-meet-google-analytics.html" title="Google Audio">integrated Google Audio Ads into Google Analytics</a>, allowing users of the Audio ad platform to track all relevant KPI's from within their GA account.
