title: Image-only email marketing campaigns - just don't!

----

date: 2007-02-11 15:15

----

text: 

As more and more well-known multi-national companies seem to be turning to email marketing for lead and customer communications, it seems that regardless of their huge budgets they're overlooking some classic best practices that are crucial in getting their message delivered and acted upon.  Subject lines, content, copy, and choice of ESP all play huge roles, but by sending <strong>all-image email campaigns </strong>to their subscribers, these companies are sabotaging all their pre-send efforts.
